LED What do the lights mean?
Ligado
  • Blinking Green: Power Box is executing the initialization process.
  • Solid Green: Power Box is ready to use.
  • Blinking Red: Upgrade Process. Do not power off or reboot!
  • Off: Power Box is off.
Sincronismo
  • Blinking Green: Power Box is trying to synchronize.
  • Solid Green: Power Box is synchronized through da WAN Ethernet or DSL.
  • Off: The DSL interface is disabled.
Internet
  • Blinking Green: Trying to get an IP address or is communicating to Internet.
  • Solid Green: Power Box received one IP address to the Internet.
  • Off: There is no Internet connection.
    		•
    HPNA
    • Blinking Green: The HPNA network is receiving or transmitting data.
    • Solid Green: The HPNA is enabled and has other nodes connected to it.
    • Off: The HPNA is disabled or no cable is connected to the interface.
    Qualidade HPNA
    • Solid Green: The HPNA network has a good quality.
    • Solid Orange: The HPNA network has a average quality.
    • Solid Red: The HPNA network has a poor quality.
    • Off: The HPNA is disabled or no cable is connected to the interface.
    TV
    • Blinking Green: Power Box is receiving or transmitting data from the TV circuit.
    • Solid Green: Power Box received one IP address from the TV circuit.
    • Off: The TV circuit is not activated.
    Wi-Fi
    • Blinking Green: The Wireless interface is receiving or transmitting data.
    • Solid Green: The Wireless interface is activated.
    • Blinking Red: The WPS negotiation is activated.
    • Off: The wireless interface is disabled.
    Telefone 1/2
    • Blinking Green: Phone is off-hook or a call is on-going.
    • Blinking Green Faster: Message not read on the voicemail.
    • Solid Green: The line is registered, you are able to make and receive calls.
    • Off: The line is disabled or unable to register.
    Button What do the buttons mean?
    WPS When you press this button, you will enable the WPS feature for a short moment. This allow new Wi-Fi devices to join in to your network without type the password.
    Reconfigurar When you keep this button pressed, you will restore the original configuration of the Power Box. This will erase all customized settings, including your SSID and Wi-Fi password.
